FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Coachii free?

Yes. Coachii is completely free for coaches, players and parents. There are no subscription fees, listing fees or booking commissions. Coaches can create a full profile and receive enquiries at no cost.

Is Coachii for coaches or for parents?

Both. Coachii is designed to connect local coaches with local players and parents. Coaches use it to build their profile and manage enquiries. Parents and players use it to discover trusted coaches nearby and get in touch directly.

Where is Coachii available?

Coachii is available across the UK's major sports cities — from Liverpool and Manchester to London, Birmingham, Glasgow, Cardiff and beyond. Search by postcode to find coaches near you.

What sports are supported?

Coachii supports a wide range of sports including football, tennis, cricket, swimming, basketball, netball, rugby, athletics, badminton, martial arts, gymnastics, hockey and more. New sports are added regularly based on demand.

How do coaches get discovered?

Coaches who create a profile on Coachii appear in local search results when parents and players search by sport and postcode. The more complete your profile, the more likely you are to appear in relevant local searches.

Is Coachii only for clubs?

No. Coachii is for individual coaches as well as coaches working within clubs. It is built for coach discovery, so parents and players can find private coaching, group sessions or club-based coaching near them.

Does Coachii take booking fees?

No. Coachii does not take any booking or transaction fees. When a parent or player enquires through Coachii and arranges a session, 100% of the session fee goes to the coach. Coachii is a discovery and communication platform, not a booking agency.
